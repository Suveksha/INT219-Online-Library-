Our Vision

We have always believed in the motto "Sharing is the Best therapy". The world is our family and sharing our resources is a gesture of showing that we care about each other. There are millions of people in this world who do not have access to books like most of us do. Hence, this website is a medium to bridge the gap between them and us. It is a way to help them escape and discover a world far from their harsh realities. A place like 'Neverland' where everything is possible.

Thus, we encourage you to share your books with the rest of the world.
